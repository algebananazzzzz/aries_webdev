About Ariesse

Ever wondered why the name??

I chose constellation names as the naming convention of my themes.

My conception of Aries symbolises passion, drive and most importantly, a powerful start to all. This is the first theme I am going to develop, and I have little idea of the challenges to be uncovered, and that is why I chose to name this theme Ariesse.

flash_on

Simple yet elegant

This theme is based on simplicity and take a minimalistic approach. I hope to create an aesthetically appealing, yet highly functional theme based on my own conception.

group

Appealing UI and UX

My themes aim to incorporate components and animations that provide more feedback to users. Additionally, the adaptation from Materializecss allows me to create a familiar theme across all platforms allow for a more unified user experience.

settings

Easy to work with

Detailed documentation, with plenty of examples to show you how you can utlize my themes in your website. Additionally, I made it compatible with other css and js libraries like granim.js and animate.css, commonly used by developers globally.
